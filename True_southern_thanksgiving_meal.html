There are many ways you can volunteer for Operation Turkey, whether it’s on Thanksgiving Day, the day before at the Turkey Tailgate, in the weeks leading up to Thanksgiving Day, or all year round! From assisting at local food banks to participating in meal delivery services, you’ll find a variety of volunteer opportunities to suit your interests and schedule. This guide will explore different avenues for volunteering, including virtual options for those unable to leave home. Operation Turkey is a 100% volunteer organization with no paid employees. We are a community of over 40,000 volunteers nationwide who work together to ensure those in need receive a warm meal on Thanksgiving Day. Watch our new video to learn how you can help! We rely on weekday teams of 12-15 volunteers to operate our soup kitchen and serve 400+ meals each day. Our Daily Bread volunteers choose the weekday that best fits their availability, with an ongoing commitment to volunteer 8AM-Noon on their day each week. Contact a food bank or food pantry near you about volunteering well before the holidays. With so many people interested in volunteering over the holidays, spots can fill up quickly and you may need to attend a volunteer orientation before you start. Be open to helping where you're needed most.
